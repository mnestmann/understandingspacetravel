Understanding the History of Space Exploration

Before beginning this lesson, take a few minutes to think about what you already know regarding space travel.

  • What were key discoveries that made space flight possible?
  • Who were the people that advanced our ability to travel into space?
  • How would you summarize the story of space travel thus far?

Reflecting on History

It's not enough to know the facts regarding space exploration. Take some time to think about how events connect on the timeline. In particular, take a few minutes to reflect on each of the following questions:

  1. How did literature/art affect mathematical discoveries?
  2. In what ways did competition promote discovery? When was cooperation necessary for advancement? What do you think is the right balance between competition and cooperation?
